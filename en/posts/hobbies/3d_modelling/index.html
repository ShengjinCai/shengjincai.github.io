My 3D Animations

3D Animation works using Blender

Since the first time I watched Zootopia at the age of 15, my interest in animation has been profound. The exceptional work of Pixar has deeply impressed me. In early 2024, I finally began using Blender as my primary modeling application and started creating my own projects with it. I studied several tutorials and have become proficient in using this software based on my own insights.

I owe a special thanks to KurTips, one of the best online tutors I’ve ever had.

Little Foxes in the Forest#

This is my debut piece. Through hands-on learning and a commitment to perfectionism, it took me approximately 8 days, working over 10 hours each day, to complete the entire artwork.

Ink Dragon#

This is my second piece. The scene in this artwork is significantly larger compared to my previous work. I utilized 3D models to create 2D animation, achieving beautiful results in the end. The style draws inspiration from traditional Chinese ink paintings known as Guohua.
